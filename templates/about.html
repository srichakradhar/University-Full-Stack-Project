About Us

Introduction

This project is developed by the Kennesaw State University team. The construction neighborhood risk mitigation tool is developed to help professionals in neighborhood risk assessment, management and planning. CNRM executes professionals’ input, social input, CSR input around a comprehensive set of risk factors while allowing them to visualize the result of modern risk assessment. The biggest advancement of this tool would be the contribution to sustainable development by mitigating all the causes of neighborhood work stops.
